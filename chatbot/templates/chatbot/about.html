Chat Bot

One of the most common applications of chatbots is customer support, where they help answer frequently asked questions (FAQs), provide troubleshooting guides, process returns, and track orders. By automating these tasks, chatbots reduce wait times and improve customer satisfaction. For example, e-commerce chatbots assist users in finding products, checking delivery status, and offering discounts.Chatbots also enhance business process automation by handling repetitive tasks such as booking appointments, sending reminders, collecting feedback, and assisting with HR processes like employee onboarding. In HR, chatbots can quickly respond to employee queries about leave policies or payroll, saving time for both employees and HR teams.
